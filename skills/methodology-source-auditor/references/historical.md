# Historical Method Audit

## Audit questions

- What archive, corpus, period, geography, or source base is visible?
- Are source survival, selection, translation, chronology, and provenance limits addressed?
- Does the argument distinguish primary source evidence from later interpretation?
- Are anachronism, presentism, and missing counter-archives considered?

## Boundaries

Use this reference when a source supports documentary, archival, chronological, or historical-interpretive claims.

## Cannot support

- Present-day prevalence without current evidence.
- Causal generalization beyond the historical case without comparative support.
- Claims of complete historical record when source survival is partial.
