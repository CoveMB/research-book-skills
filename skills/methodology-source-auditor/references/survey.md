# Survey Method Audit

## Audit questions

- Is the sampling frame, recruitment method, response rate, and weighting visible?
- Are question wording, scales, field dates, and mode effects reported?
- Are construct validity, nonresponse, and coverage bias addressed?
- Does the sample match the population named in the manuscript claim?

## Boundaries

Use this reference when a source supports claims about attitudes, self-reports, prevalence, or group comparisons from survey data.

## Cannot support

- Causal claims without an appropriate design.
- Population claims when the sample frame is mismatched or unavailable.
- Fine subgroup claims without visible sample size and uncertainty.
