# Experimental Method Audit

## Audit questions

- Are randomization, controls, allocation, blinding, power, attrition, and protocol deviations visible?
- Are outcomes pre-specified, measured reliably, and reported with uncertainty?
- Is the setting ecologically valid for the manuscript claim?
- Are harms, exclusions, and missing data handled transparently?

## Boundaries

Use this reference when randomized, controlled, laboratory, field, or intervention studies support causal or comparative claims.

## Cannot support

- Claims outside the tested population, intervention, comparator, or outcome.
- Long-term effects not measured by the experiment.
- Policy-scale claims without external-validity discussion.
