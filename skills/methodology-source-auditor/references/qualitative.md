# Qualitative Method Audit

## Audit questions

- Is the sampling strategy visible, and does it fit the claim?
- Are coding, memoing, interview, observation, or document-analysis procedures described?
- Are positionality, triangulation, negative cases, and transferability addressed where relevant?
- Are participant quotes or field notes separated from the author's interpretation?

## Boundaries

Use this reference only when a qualitative source is expected to support a claim about meanings, practices, experience, interpretation, institutions, or social processes.

## Cannot support

- Population prevalence without an appropriate quantitative design.
- Strong causal claims unless the design directly supports causal process tracing.
- Universal claims beyond the sampled context without explicit transferability limits.
