# Legal Method Audit

## Audit questions

- What jurisdiction, authority level, date, and procedural posture are visible?
- Is the source binding authority, persuasive authority, commentary, policy, or advocacy?
- Does the cited rule still apply, and are later amendments or cases relevant?
- Does the source fit the doctrinal question being asked?

## Boundaries

Use this reference when legal authority, doctrine, regulation, policy, or legal interpretation is central to source use.

## Cannot support

- Legal clearance or advice.
- Claims outside the named jurisdiction or date range.
- Empirical claims about legal effects without empirical evidence.
